Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird is a game I made using OOP. It is very similar to the original game where you go between the pipes to get points, but if you hit the ground or a pipe you lose. You can use the space bar to fly.

2048

2048 is a game where you need to try to get a high score. You can merge same number tiles together using the arrow keys and score points depending on the value of the tile you merged. You lose when the board gets completely filled and you have no possible moves.

Space Invaders

This version of Space Invaders is slightly different from the original. You can choose the number of aliens that will appear on your screen, and your goal is to shoot the aliens, getting a point every time you hit one, which you can do by firing a bullet with your spacebar. They will travel from left to right, slowly descending every time they hit a wall.

Snake Game

Snake Game is one of my best PyGame projects. There are multiple gamemodes. You can play normal, with the option to choose how many apples come on screen, and a multiplayer version with two snakes. Your goal is to score points by moving your arrow keys and eating the apples that appear on screen. Every time you eat an apple, you get longer, and if you hit yourself or a wall you lose.
