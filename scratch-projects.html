Brawl Stars

Brawl Stars which I made in Scratch is quite similar to the real game but much simpler. It is not fully complete yet, but gameplay for a few brawlers is done along with supers which also work, as well as a working Starr Drop system, however Enemy AI is not complete.

Survivor

Survivor is a game I made as a school project. It is quite similar to the game Vampire Survivors, where you have different weapons that activate over time which kill repeatedly spawning waves of enemies. After a certain period of time, a boss will spawn, and after you beat the boss you will get a new weapon and go to the next level. There are 3 levels.

Gun Blitz

Gun Blitz is a very simple game I made a long time ago. It involves a player that can shoot bullets at a enemy that repeatedly spawn which also shoot at you. When you kill a enemy, it gives you one point. There is also a version with hordes of less powerful zombies that spawn instead.

Corrupted Robots

Corrupted Robots is a project I made for my capstone school project in grade 5 which was about corruption in Zimbabwe. This game was part of a fundraising project, which was the call to action of my presentation, where I made videos and also this game, and I raised over 1400 usd. In this game, you shoot robots that damage you and after each round answer questions about corruption.
